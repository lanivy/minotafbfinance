Minot AFB Finance FAQ

How do I check my BAH rate?

Visit https://www.defensetravel.dod.mil/site/bah.cfm and input your rank and zip code (58705 for Minot AFB).

When is military pay day?

Military pay is usually issued on the 1st and 15th of each month. If these dates fall on a weekend or holiday, payment is processed on the previous business day.

How do I update my direct deposit information?

You can update your direct deposit info via the Defense Finance and Accounting Service (DFAS) myPay website at https://mypay.dfas.mil.

What is the process for travel reimbursement?

Submit your travel voucher through the Defense Travel System (DTS). Make sure to keep all receipts and follow your unit’s instructions.

Who do I contact for pay discrepancies?

Contact your local finance office or submit a pay inquiry through DFAS myPay.

How can I access my Leave and Earnings Statement (LES)?

Access your LES through the DFAS myPay portal at https://mypay.dfas.mil.

What should I do if I lose my CAC card?

Report the loss immediately to your unit security manager and visit the nearest RAPIDS site to request a replacement.

How do I request a military pay advance?

Contact your local finance office for the application process and eligibility requirements.

What forms are required for a Permanent Change of Station (PCS)?

You will typically need orders, travel vouchers, and receipts. Consult your finance office for complete details.

Where can I find information about special pays and allowances?

Visit the official Air Force Personnel Center (AFPC) website or contact your finance office.

How do I report an error on my LES?

Submit a pay inquiry through DFAS myPay or contact your finance office directly.

Can I access my finance records remotely?

Yes, through secure government portals such as DFAS myPay and Defense Travel System (DTS).

How do I enroll in the Thrift Savings Plan (TSP)?

Enrollment can be done via the myPay website or through your personnel office.

What is the deadline for submitting travel vouchers?

Generally, travel vouchers must be submitted within 5 days after completion of travel. Check with your unit for specific rules.

How do I get assistance with budgeting and financial planning?

Contact the Financial Management office or the base Financial Readiness program.

What is a Leave and Earnings Statement (LES)?

LES is a monthly statement showing your pay, leave balances, and deductions.

How can I update my personal information for pay purposes?

Update your information via DFAS myPay or your unit personnel office.

What do I do if I have questions about my retirement pay?

Contact the Defense Finance and Accounting Service (DFAS) Retired and Annuitant Pay office.

How do I submit a Pay Inquiry?

Use the DFAS myPay website or contact your finance office directly for assistance.

What is the Defense Travel System (DTS)?

DTS is the online system used for travel authorization and reimbursement.

如何查询我的住房补贴（BAH）标准？

请访问 https://www.defensetravel.dod.mil/site/bah.cfm，输入军衔和邮编（Minot AFB 为 58705）。

军人发薪日是什么时候？

军人薪水通常在每月的1日和15日发放。如果遇到周末或假日，会提前到最近的工作日发放。

如何更新我的直接存款信息？

您可以通过国防财务会计服务（DFAS）的 myPay 网站更新直接存款信息，网址是 https://mypay.dfas.mil。

差旅报销的流程是什么？

请通过国防差旅系统（DTS）提交差旅报销单，保存所有收据，并按照单位要求操作。

如果我的工资有问题，我应该联系谁？

请联系您当地的财务办公室，或者通过DFAS myPay提交工资查询。

如何获取我的请假和收入报表（LES）？

通过DFAS myPay门户网站访问您的LES，网址是 https://mypay.dfas.mil。

如果我丢失了CAC卡怎么办？

请立即报告给您的单位安全管理员，并前往最近的RAPIDS中心申请补发。

如何申请军人预支工资？

请联系当地财务办公室了解申请流程和资格要求。

调动（PCS）需要哪些表格？

通常需要调令、差旅报销单和收据。具体请咨询财务办公室。

在哪里可以查询特殊津贴和补助信息？

请访问空军人员中心（AFPC）官网，或者咨询您的财务办公室。

如何报告我的LES错误？

您可以通过DFAS myPay提交工资查询，或者直接联系财务办公室。

我可以远程访问我的财务记录吗？

可以，通过安全的政府门户网站如DFAS myPay和国防差旅系统（DTS）。

如何参加节约储蓄计划（TSP）？

您可以通过myPay网站或人事办公室报名。

提交差旅报销单的截止日期是什么？

通常差旅结束后5天内提交。具体规则请咨询单位。

我如何获得预算和财务规划帮助？

请联系财务管理办公室或基地财务准备项目。

什么是请假和收入报表（LES）？

LES是每月显示您工资、请假余额和扣款的报表。

如何更新薪酬相关的个人信息？

通过DFAS myPay或人事办公室更新信息。

如果我对退休工资有疑问怎么办？

请联系国防财务会计服务（DFAS）退休工资办公室。

如何提交工资查询？

请使用DFAS myPay网站或直接联系财务办公室。

什么是国防差旅系统（DTS）？

DTS是在线差旅授权和报销系统。
